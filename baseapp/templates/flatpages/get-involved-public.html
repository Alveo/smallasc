How can I get involved ?

WANTED
Australian English speakers

 

If you are over 18 and you have completed all your schooling (from kindergarten through to high school) in Australia, you can be part of the national collection of Australian English accents.

You will be recorded reading some words and sentences, having a conversation and playing a game with another participant. You will also be asked to complete a short questionnaire.

Duration = 3 one-hour sessions (on separate days).

Reimbursement: You will be given $25 for the first and second recording session and $40 for the final recording session.

Your data will be kept anonymously, but you will have a code so you can access your own speech data in the final database on the web.

If you would like to participate, you can register your interest here.
